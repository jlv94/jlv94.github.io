Why should you work with a fractional CMO in the first place?

A Fractional Chief Marketing Officer (fCMO) is a senior marketing executive who works on a part-time or contract basis with businesses, usually small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs).
As a fractional CMO, I will provide leadership and expertise on projects without the long-term commitment or expense associated with a permanent hire. This role allows businesses to access high-level marketing skills, develop tailored strategies, and implement effective marketing initiatives while maintaining flexibility and cost efficiency.

What are the benefits for SMBs to work with me as a fractional CMO?

  • Cost-Effective Expertise: I provide high-level marketing leadership and execution at a fraction of the cost of a full-time CMO.
  • Flexibility and Scalability: as your business needs evolve, I can adjust my involvement accordingly. Whether you need part-time support during a busy season or a more hands-on approach for a specific project, the flexibility is unmatched.
  • Tailored for your needs and size: whether you are multitasking between owner and marketer or already have a marketing team in place, I will backup your marketing effort and fit to your company needs and size.
  • Tools and Technology Expertise: Benefit from my extensive experience with CRM, ERP, data visualization, emailing plateforms, Google Analytics, scripting language—and more! I can quickly implement and train your team on essential tools!
  • Focus on Core Business: By outsourcing your marketing leadership, you can concentrate on what you do best—running your business. I will take on the marketing burden, allowing you to focus on operations and growth.
