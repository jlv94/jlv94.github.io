Why should you work with a fractional CMO in the first place?

A Fractional Chief Marketing Officer (fCMO) is a senior marketing executive who works on a part-time or contract basis with businesses, usually small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs). As a fractional CMO, I will provide leadership and expertise on projects without the long-term commitment or expense associated with a permanent hire. This role allows businesses to access high-level marketing skills, develop tailored strategies, and implement effective marketing initiatives while maintaining flexibility and cost efficiency.
